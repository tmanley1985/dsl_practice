ReorderRule for SKU 98765
When StockLevel < (3 months sales velocity)
OrderQuantity: 4 months sales velocity

Description: For high-demand items (SKU 98765), this rule suggests ordering more aggressively to take advantage of bulk purchase discounts or to ensure supply chain stability. It's triggered when stock levels drop below the sales velocity of the past three months, with the order quantity set to the projected sales velocity of the next four months.
